Project Summary

This project is part of a lecture in the Faculty of Engineering. The course was about developing a device that has input and output. To make our own tangible drum device, we created BDrum, a drum device with touch sensors. We created touch sensors with aluminum sheets and resistors. This device has four modes, drumset, piano, percussion, and Japanese instruments, and players can change the modes with a button.

My role

As a junior undergraduate student, I worked on this project with a teammate. I was responsible for creating a circuit diagram and designing a circuit board with Eagle. I printed out the board and made our own circuit board. Also, I played a major role in creating the device: cut, assembled, and soldered.
